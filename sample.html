Basin mixers are the one tap that can do it all, bringing together both hot and cold water so you can find your perfect balance. One simple tap delivers water to your basin making temperature and pressure adjustment easy.

Extended Basin Mixers

Extended basin mixers are ideal for taller or above-counter basins, with a heightened profile that forms the centrepiece of your vanity space. When youve got a statement basin on display that you want to highlight, look for an extended basin mixer.

If youre crafting a classic look, consider a basin tap set instead. These three-piece sets feature separate hot and cold taps. However, their style isnt limited to traditional spaces; theyre available in a range of finishes like modern matte black that mean theyre at home in any bathroom.


Beyond the Basin

Taps and mixers can be a great way to create a cohesive look in your bathroom by choosing complementary products. Matching your basin mixer with a shower mixer doesnt just look great, it brings a common sense of functionality to the whole room. Twin shower mixers are another alternative that give you separate control over hot and cold temperatures.

If youve chosen a basin tap set, consider a matching shower set that brings that style through to your shower space.
